Compliance for Australian general practice

Compliance that reads the record, not just the checklist.

Certika is an AI-assisted compliance platform for Australian general practice. It reviews clinical documentation and billing against RACGP Standards and MBS requirements — and every finding is checked by a clinician before it counts.

Documentation

Clinical notes assessed for adequacy against the RACGP Standards.

Billing

MBS item alignment, to reduce avoidable Medicare audit exposure.

Clinician-verified

A clinician reviews and signs off every finding. Nothing is acted on unread.

Certika is founded by an Australian healthcare practitioner and general-practice owner, and built around how accreditation and Medicare compliance actually work inside a busy practice.

It is designed for Australian data sovereignty from the ground up, and aligned to the RACGP Standards (6th edition). The platform is in active development.
